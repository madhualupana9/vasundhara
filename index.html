Why Invest in Maheshwaram?

Infrastructure & Connectivity

Strategic Road Links: Proximity to Srisailam Highway (NH 765), Bangalore Highway (NH-44), and the Outer Ring Road ensures seamless connectivity.

Upcoming Expressway: The proposed six-lane expressway, Ratan Tata Road, will further enhance accessibility.

Industrial & Employment Hubs

Hyderabad Pharma City: A massive 19,000-acre pharmaceutical hub under development.

Electronic Hardware Park: An exclusive 339.75-acre park for electronic manufacturing.

Real Estate Growth

Appreciation Trends: Over the past five years, property values have increased by 10–15% annually.

Affordable Entry Point: Land rates start at approximately ₹3,500 per square foot.

Social Infrastructure

Educational Institutions: Close to renowned schools and universities.

Healthcare Facilities: Access to major hospitals like Apollo and Yashoda.
